App 名称（原创拟人化创造词）：
Borrily

English Description
Borrily is your kind and dependable borrowing companion. Log what you lent, to whom, and when it’s due. When the time comes, tap once to generate a thoughtful, polite message—crafted by on-device AI—to gently ask for your item back. No notifications, no cloud, just quiet help when you need it.
